# Non-Tribal Culture

Organizations become tribes by default.

Tribal dynamics are not moral failures.

They are coordination shortcuts.

They are also capture vectors.

This module treats non-tribal culture as anti-capture engineering.

---

## Tribes form around power

Tribes form around:

- leaders
- domains
- prestige
- and scarcity

When scarcity exists, factions compete.

Faction competition is not automatically bad.

It becomes fatal when it replaces:

- truth
- repair
- and shared constraints

---

## The organizational hazard

The hazard is factional capture.

Factional capture occurs when:

- truth becomes loyalty
- disagreement becomes betrayal
- and repair becomes ammunition

This produces internal civil war.

---

## Failure modes

### Loyalty reality

Facts are faction-dependent.

Coordination collapses.

### Enemy manufacturing

Teams require internal enemies.

This stabilizes hatred.

### Purity tests

Belonging requires ideological alignment.

This destroys contradiction.

### Leadership cults

Leaders become sacred.

This creates immunity.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable organization must preserve:

1. truth regimes that survive faction pressure
2. shared repair obligations across factions
3. constraints that apply symmetrically
4. incentives that punish loyalty-over-truth behavior

If the organization becomes tribal, capture becomes stable.
