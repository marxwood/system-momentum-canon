# Abuse and Misuse

Every capability can be misused.

Misuse is not a moral anomaly.

It is a predictable outcome of:

- incentives
- power
- and access

This module treats misuse as a design input.

---

## Misuse is not only external

Misuse can be:

- external attack
- insider abuse
- or factional weaponization

Organizations that model only external threats are blind.

Blindness produces capture.

---

## The organizational hazard

The hazard is capability without constraint.

When capability expands faster than constraints:

- misuse becomes inevitable
- and detection becomes too late

---

## Failure modes

### Rogue execution

Capabilities are used outside intent.

This produces harm and denial.

### Delegated laundering

Misuse is performed through intermediaries.

Accountability collapses.

### Incentive misuse

People misuse because it is rewarded.

This becomes stable corruption.

### Normalization

Small misuse becomes normal.

Normalization produces systemic capture.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable organization must preserve:

1. misuse scenarios for critical capabilities
2. constraints that prevent unbounded action
3. detection and auditability for high-impact actions
4. repair ownership following misuse

If misuse is not modeled, capability becomes a liability.
