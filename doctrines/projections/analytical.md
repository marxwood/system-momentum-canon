# Analytical Projections — Doctrinal View

Analytical Projections exist to support
observation, evaluation, and understanding.

They look backward, sideways, and abstractly —
never forward with authority.

---

## Purpose

Analytical projections exist to:

- analyze outcomes
- detect patterns
- evaluate performance
- support reflection

They are inputs to thinking,
not triggers for action.

---

## What Analytical Projections May Contain

- metrics
- summaries
- trend analyses
- comparisons
- derived indicators

They may contradict operational views
without implying error.

---

## What Analytical Projections Must Never Do

- decide
- govern
- commit
- redefine meaning
- override memory

Analytics that silently drive action
create unaccountable power.

---

## Relationship to Reflection

Analytical projections often feed
the Reflective Domain.

They provide **evidence**, not conclusions.

Reflection interprets.
Governance decides.

---

## Typical Failure Modes

- metric fixation
- optimizing for indicators
- confusing correlation with causation
- letting analytics dictate policy

Most modern failures
are analytical projections mistaken for truth.

---

## Closing Note

Analytics are powerful precisely because
they simplify reality.

Forgetting that simplification
is where danger begins.
