# Evidence Primitives

A civilization cannot coordinate on “truth”.

But it can coordinate on evidence primitives.

Evidence primitives are not conclusions.

They are anchors.

They are the smallest units of reality contact that can survive:

- distance
- complexity
- propaganda
- tribal incentives
- time

This doctrine does not require everyone to agree.

It requires enough actors to share enough anchors to preserve correction capacity.

---

## Why evidence primitives matter

At scale, most claims are not evaluated.

They are adopted.

They are adopted through:

- authority
- identity
- fear
- convenience
- narrative resonance

This creates a predictable failure mode:

Civilizations become systems where:

- evidence is optional
- credibility is theater
- contradiction is hostility

Evidence primitives exist to prevent this collapse.

---

## What an evidence primitive is

An evidence primitive is a unit that can be:

- independently checked (at least by some actors)
- preserved over time
- attributed to a source
- compared against alternatives
- used to correct a belief

It is not required to be perfect.

It is required to be inspectable.

---

## What an evidence primitive is not

An evidence primitive is not:

- a moral claim
- a political slogan
- a social permission signal
- a story
- an identity statement
- a conclusion disguised as a fact

Civilizations collapse when they treat conclusions as primitives.

---

## The minimal set

A correction-capable society needs a minimal set of primitives.

Not a comprehensive ontology.

A minimal set.

### 1) Observable events

Something happened.

Not what it means.
Not why it happened.

Only: that it occurred.

### 2) Source attribution

Who is asserting it.

Not whether they are “good”.

Only: who is responsible for the claim.

### 3) Timestamp and context

When and where the claim is situated.

Not for bureaucracy.

For comparability.

### 4) Traceable artifacts

Records that can be inspected.

Not summaries.

Not screenshots without provenance.

Artifacts.

### 5) Contradiction allowance

The ability to hold two incompatible claims without immediate purge.

This is not relativism.

It is the precondition to correction.

---

## The operational discipline

The discipline is:

**Build public claims from primitives upward.**

Not from conclusions downward.

This is how correction survives.

---

## Failure modes

### Evidence inflation

Everything becomes “evidence”.

Emotional reactions.
Viral posts.
Moral outrage.
Authority statements.

When everything is evidence, nothing is.

### Evidence starvation

Nothing is checkable.

Everything is hidden behind:

- secrecy
- complexity
- classification
- paywalls
- intimidation

When nothing is checkable, power becomes truth.

### Evidence theater

Evidence is performed, not provided.

Actors learn to display the aesthetic of evidence:

- graphs without sources
- documents without provenance
- citations without inspectability

This creates a civilization where credibility is cosplay.

---

## Minimal implementation

A society does not need to agree on ideology.

It needs to preserve three abilities:

1. to produce inspectable primitives
2. to dispute interpretations without annihilation
3. to revise beliefs without humiliation

If these abilities survive, correction survives.

If they die, coercion becomes the stabilizer.
