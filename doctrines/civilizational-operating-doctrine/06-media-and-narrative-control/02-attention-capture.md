# Attention Capture

At civilizational scale, attention is not a resource.

Attention is a weapon.

Whoever controls attention can:

- define what is real
- define what is urgent
- define what is moral
- define what is permitted

This is why attention capture is not a side effect.

It is a governance tool.

---

## Attention is the bottleneck

Evidence primitives can exist.

Provenance can exist.

Contradiction capacity can exist.

But if attention is captured, none of it matters.

Captured attention produces a society where:

- visibility replaces evidence
- urgency replaces consequence mapping
- emotion replaces accountability
- performance replaces correction

---

## The civilizational hazard

The hazard is not distraction.

The hazard is attention capture being engineered to:

- raise arousal
- compress time
- remove nuance
- and make coercion feel necessary

Arousal is the enemy of correction.

Arousal makes boundaries feel intolerable.

---

## How attention capture works

Attention capture is produced through:

- outrage cycles
- humiliation rituals
- fear saturation
- constant emergency framing
- novelty addiction
- moral intoxication loops

These are not accidental.

They are stable incentive equilibria.

---

## Failure modes

### Arousal governance

Society is governed through emotional arousal.

This produces:

- panic decisions
- loyalty tests
- enemy inflation

### Speed collapse

Everything must be decided immediately.

This disables:

- intent declaration
- consequence mapping
- accountability ledgers

### Noise saturation

Contradiction becomes noise.

Evidence becomes invisible.

This produces apathy reality.

Apathy reality is stable.

It is also capturable.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable order must preserve:

1. spaces where attention is not captured
2. time for consequence mapping
3. permission for uncertainty and contradiction

If attention capture dominates, coercion becomes the stabilizer.
