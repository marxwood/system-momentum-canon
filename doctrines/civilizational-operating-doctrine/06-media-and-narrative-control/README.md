# Media and Narrative Control

At civilizational scale, media is not “information”.

Media is coordination infrastructure.

It determines:

- what becomes visible
- what becomes discussable
- what becomes dangerous to say
- what becomes socially rewarded
- what becomes punishable

Narratives are not stories.

Narratives are control surfaces.

They shape legitimacy and action permissions.

---

## The core stance

The stance of this section is:

**Narrative is an interface.**

It is not truth.

It is not morality.

It is not identity.

It is a coordination mechanism that can be:

- built
- captured
- weaponized
- repaired

---

## The civilizational hazard

The hazard is not that narratives exist.

Narratives must exist.

The hazard is that narrative control replaces:

- evidence primitives
- provenance and integrity
- contradiction capacity
- uncertainty tolerance

When narrative replaces these, society becomes:

- emotionally coordinated
- and correction-incapable

Correction becomes socially illegal.

Coercion becomes the stabilizer.

---

## What this section covers

This section defines:

- narratives as control surfaces
- attention capture as weapon
- humiliation as a governance tool
- reality fragmentation
- epistemic pollution
- censorship and self-censorship
- credibility theater
- resistance without becoming propaganda

This is not a media critique.

It is an operational doctrine for preserving correction capacity.

---

## Failure modes this section defends against

This section exists because media systems collapse into stable forms:

- propaganda coordination
- humiliation rituals
- outrage economies
- silence equilibria
- cynicism and apathy
- and reality fragmentation

These forms can maintain order.

They cannot maintain correction.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable order must preserve:

1. evidence anchors that survive narrative pressure
2. contradiction without humiliation
3. credibility systems that can be corrected

If narrative becomes the only coordination layer, coercion will become the stabilizer.
