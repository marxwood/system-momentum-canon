# Credibility Theater

When evidence primitives and provenance collapse, credibility does not disappear.

It mutates.

It becomes theater.

Credibility theater is the performance of truth without inspectability.

It allows society to feel:

- informed
- moral
- aligned
- and justified

while losing correction capacity.

---

## Why theater is attractive

Theater is attractive because it is cheaper than correction.

Correction requires:

- inspection
- contradiction
- uncertainty tolerance
- and belief update

Theater requires:

- symbols
- credentials
- aesthetics
- and loyalty signals

Theater scales.

Correction is expensive.

---

## The civilizational hazard

The hazard is not that people trust authorities.

Trust is necessary.

The hazard is that authority becomes immune to correction.

When authority is immune, it becomes power.

When power becomes credibility, society loses reality contact.

---

## How credibility theater works

Credibility theater is produced through:

- citations without inspectability
- documents without provenance
- experts as loyalty signals
- statistics as aesthetic objects
- fact-checking as factional weaponry
- rituals of “being informed”

The performance replaces the substrate.

---

## Failure modes

### Credential monopoly

Credentials become the only permitted evidence.

This produces authority reality.

### Factional expertise

Experts become tribal assets.

Contradiction becomes betrayal.

This produces tribal reality.

### Exhaustion collapse

Actors stop attempting to inspect anything.

They outsource reality completely.

This produces apathy reality.

Apathy reality is stable.

It is also capturable.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable order must preserve:

1. inspectable anchors that are not status-based
2. provenance that cannot be replaced by credentials
3. contradiction capacity that can contest authority

Without these, credibility becomes theater.

And theater becomes governance.
