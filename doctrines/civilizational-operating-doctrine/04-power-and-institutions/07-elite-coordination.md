# Elite Coordination

Civilizations do not collapse only from below.

They collapse from above.

Elites coordinate.

They always coordinate.

The question is not whether elite coordination exists.

The question is what it coordinates toward:

- correction-capable order
- or capture-capable stability

---

## Elite coordination is not conspiracy

Elite coordination is not necessarily secret.

It is structural.

Actors with power must coordinate because:

- they share risks
- they share dependence networks
- they share control over punishment
- they share fear of instability

This doctrine treats elite coordination as a normal civilizational function.

Not as moral pathology.

---

## The civilizational hazard

The hazard is when elite coordination becomes:

- immune to accountability ledgers
- protected by selective enforcement
- stabilized by dependence capture
- justified by collapse panic

When this happens, institutions become machines of permanent capture.

---

## How elite coordination stabilizes capture

Elite coordination stabilizes capture through:

### 1) Coalition protection

Elites protect each other from accountability.

This makes capture survivable.

### 2) Dependence management

Elites control:

- jobs
- contracts
- permits
- access
- enforcement

This makes contradiction expensive.

### 3) Narrative alignment

Elites align on narratives that justify:

- emergency measures
- selective enforcement
- suppression of contradiction

### 4) Controlled opposition

Elites permit opposition that is:

- symbolic
- containable
- and non-corrective

This produces the appearance of correction without correction.

---

## Failure modes

### Elite fragmentation

Elites split into factions.

Factional conflict turns institutions into weapons.

This produces instability and violence risk.

### Elite monopoly

Elites unify around capture.

This produces stability and decay.

### Elite panic

Elites believe instability is imminent.

They escalate coercion preemptively.

This produces permanent emergency.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable order must preserve one discipline:

**Elite coordination must be constrained by inspectable accountability.**

If elite coordination is immune, institutions are already captured.
