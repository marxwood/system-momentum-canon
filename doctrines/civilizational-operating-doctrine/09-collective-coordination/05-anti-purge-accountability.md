# Anti-Purge Accountability

Civilizations cannot repair without accountability.

Civilizations cannot survive if accountability becomes purge.

This module exists to prevent the most common repair failure:

repair attempts that reproduce coercion.

Anti-purge accountability is the discipline of enforcing responsibility while preserving contradiction capacity.

---

## Purge is the enemy of repair

Purge feels like justice.

Purge produces closure.

Closure stabilizes.

Closure also destroys:

- uncertainty tolerance
- contradiction capacity
- and survivable belief update

A society that repairs through purge becomes capturable again.

Often immediately.

---

## The civilizational hazard

The hazard is accountability being processed through:

- humiliation
- collective guilt
- and annihilation logic

These are emotionally satisfying.

They are operationally fatal.

They create permanent enemies.

Permanent enemies stabilize coercive order.

---

## What anti-purge accountability requires

Anti-purge accountability requires:

- evidence primitives
- provenance and integrity
- accountability ledgers
- bounded punishment gradients
- repair obligations
- and protection for contradiction

Without these, accountability becomes factional weaponry.

---

## Failure modes

### Accountability as revenge

Revenge replaces repair.

Retaliation loops become inevitable.

### Accountability as humiliation theater

Punishment becomes performance.

Performance produces fear and silence.

### Accountability asymmetry

Friends are immune.

Enemies are punished.

This produces capture.

### Accountability inflation

Accountability expands beyond responsible actors.

Collective guilt becomes normal.

This produces fear governance.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable order must preserve:

1. evidence before punishment
2. bounded consequences (no annihilation)
3. repair as requirement
4. contradiction protection

If accountability cannot be separated from purge, repair will reproduce coercion.
