# Anti-Rationalization

At civilizational scale, rationalization is unlimited.

This is why legitimacy is capturable.

If a society allows after-the-fact justification to function as legitimacy, then:

- any action can be justified
- any boundary can be erased
- any contradiction can be punished
- any coercion can be reframed as necessity

Anti-rationalization is the discipline that prevents legitimacy from becoming a license.

---

## Rationalization is not lying

Rationalization is not simply deception.

Rationalization is a coordination mechanism.

It allows actors to:

- act first
- justify later
- and demand obedience retroactively

This is how coercion becomes normal without explicit ideology.

---

## The civilizational hazard

The hazard is not that rationalization exists.

It will always exist.

The hazard is that rationalization becomes:

- socially rewarded
- institutionally protected
- and immune to accountability ledgers

When rationalization becomes immune, correction dies.

---

## The doctrine rule

The doctrine rule is:

**Justification is not legitimacy.**

Legitimacy requires:

- declared intent
- bounded action
- accountable ledgers
- consequence mapping
- repair mechanisms

Without these, justification is narrative.

Narrative can coordinate.

Narrative cannot correct.

---

## The main rationalization engines

This doctrine does not enumerate ideologies.

It enumerates engines.

### 1) Emergency engine

“Extraordinary times require extraordinary measures.”

### 2) Enemy engine

“They forced us.”

### 3) Destiny engine

“History demanded it.”

### 4) Purity engine

“Our side is morally exempt.”

### 5) Futurity engine

“We are building a better future, therefore anything is permitted now.”

These engines are not always false.

They are always dangerous.

---

## Failure modes

### Retroactive legitimacy

Action is taken first.

Legitimacy is constructed later.

This destroys accountability ledgers.

### Moral licensing

Actors believe their intent sanctifies their actions.

This erases boundaries.

### Narrative immunity

Certain actors become immune to contradiction.

This produces capture.

### Rationalization escalation

Each justification requires stronger closure.

This produces increasing coercion.

---

## Minimal implementation

A correction-capable order must preserve one non-negotiable discipline:

**No legitimacy without pre-commitment.**

Pre-commitment means:

- intent declared before action
- boundaries accepted before action
- accountability accepted before action

Without pre-commitment, legitimacy becomes storytelling.

And storytelling becomes coercion.
