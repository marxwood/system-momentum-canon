# Canonical Core Audit (Draft)

This document records the **audit state** of the System Momentum Canon.

This document is **non-canonical**.
It introduces no meaning and confers no authority.

---

## Audit Scope

Audit applies to:

- all canonical documents under `core/`
- canonical hierarchy and ordering
- internal consistency and boundary integrity

Audit does not evaluate:

- correctness of ideas
- external applicability
- derivative interpretations

---

## Audit Criteria

Audit evaluates the Core Canon against the following criteria:

1. **Ontological completeness**

   - Canon defines what it is and what it is not
   - No dependency on repository mechanics or institutions

2. **Misuse resistance**

   - External capture vectors addressed
   - Internal misuse vectors addressed
   - Power, morality, intelligence, decision substitution prohibited

3. **Epistemic boundaries**

   - No factual, predictive, or epistemic claims
   - Explicit handling of silence and incompleteness

4. **Operational separation**

   - Clear boundary between Canon and derivatives
   - No governance or publishing rules embedded in Canon

5. **Hierarchical integrity**

   - no internal contradictions
   - structural conflict resolution
   - no interpretive balancing

6. **Closure and stability**
   - explicit closure claims (if any)
   - externalized change mechanisms
   - no incremental expansion via backdoors
